Welcome to City Centre Living!

If you live at Unite House, Culver House or Colston Street you are part of our City Centre Living Group.
This app allows you to browse information about what's on during Welcome Week. You can see who your Senior Residents are and how to contact them, what a JCR is and how to get involved, and links to all sorts of other useful information.
